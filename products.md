gold:
    Crisis-resistant investment
    Ideal for building wealth
    Green Gold: Ethical and sustainable in extraction, transportation and refining.
    Certified by LBMA

silver:
    Crisis-resistant investment in physical silver
    Ideal for building wealth
    High demand by industries
    VAT exempt (19%) through storage in a duty-free warehouse in Switzerland.

platinum:
    Rare & in Demand – Limited supply (mainly from South Africa & Russia) with increasing demand in the automotive, hydrogen, and jewelry industries.
    Inflation Protection – As stable in value as gold, protects against currency depreciation & economic crises.
    Undervalued – Previously more expensive than gold, currently cheaper with high growth potential.

palladium:
    Essential for the Automotive Industry – Key component in catalytic converters for reducing emissions, especially in gasoline engines.
    Supply Shortages – Limited production (mainly from Russia & South Africa) while demand continues to rise.
    Strong Pricing – Palladium has outperformed gold & platinum in recent years and remains scarce.
